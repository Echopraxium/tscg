Governance Trinity
Three structurally necessary and sufficient components ensuring trustworthy autonomous AI action. Removing any one causes governance collapse.
Components
● ASTP
Agent Safety Trust Protocol — wraps every task request with mandatory Intent-ID, Risk-Profile, Liability-Owner and cryptographic signature. Solves Intent Drift.
● Guardian Ensemble
Dynamic multi-agent judiciary. Adjudicates risk via collective deliberation. Hard-Block (non-appealable) or Soft-Block (pending review). Solves Governance Collapse.
● Immutable Ledger
Cryptographically chained append-only record of every governance event. Full chain of custody. Solves Liability Diffusion.
