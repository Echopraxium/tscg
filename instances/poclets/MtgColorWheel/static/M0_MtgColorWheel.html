MTG Color Wheel — TSCG Poclet

The Magic: The Gathering Color Wheel is one of the most elegant philosophical frameworks in game design: five primary colors, ten bicolor guilds, and the Artifact pseudo-color as colorless neutral substrate. Designed in 1993, stable for 30+ years.

ASFID (0.65, 0.80, 0.45, 0.85, 0.50) · REVOI (0.90, 0.85, 0.70, 0.75, 0.90) · δ₁ = 0.17 Liminal

Primary Colors

White (W) — Order & Collective

Pentagon position 0 (top) · Allies: Blue, Green · Enemies: Black, Red

Values: Structure · Peace · Community · Absolute Morality
Methods: Laws, Institutions, Collective Work, Discipline
Excesses: Conformism, Dogmatism, Sacrifice of the Individual
Mechanics: Life gain, protection, small creatures, removal

"The many are stronger than the one. Order brings peace."

Blue (U) — Perfection & Knowledge

Pentagon position 1 · Allies: White, Black · Enemies: Red, Green

Values: Knowledge · Perfection · Progress · Intellect
Methods: Study, Technology, Magic, Manipulation
Excesses: Coldness, Manipulation, Perfectionism
Mechanics: Card draw, counterspells, flying, artifact affinity

"Everything can be improved. Knowledge is power."

Black (B) — Ambition & Individual

Pentagon position 2 · Allies: Blue, Red · Enemies: White, Green

Values: Power · Achievement · Pragmatism
Methods: Power at any cost, Resource exploitation
Excesses: Egoism, Cynicism, Parasitism
Mechanics: Destruction, discard, reanimation, sacrifice

"Every being has the right to do whatever it takes. Death is a resource."

Red (R) — Freedom & Passion

Pentagon position 3 · Allies: Black, Green · Enemies: White, Blue

Values: Freedom · Emotion · Spontaneity · Action
Methods: Intuition, Passion, Creative Destruction
Excesses: Chaos, Impulsivity, Anarchy
Mechanics: Direct damage, haste, chaos, artifact destruction

"Feel, don't think. Act now, worry later."

Green (G) — Nature & Growth

Pentagon position 4 · Allies: Red, White · Enemies: Blue, Black

Values: Nature · Tradition · Organic Growth · Destiny
Methods: Adaptation, Ecosystem Reliance, Acceptance
Excesses: Fatalism, Progress Rejection, Law of the Jungle
Mechanics: Large creatures, mana acceleration, enchantments

"Everything has its place. Growth through time, not force."

Allied Guilds (Adjacent Pairs)

Adjacent colors share philosophical DNA — their guild identities reinforce each other.

Azorius (W+U)

Ravnica: The Senate — legislative body
Synthesis: Law + Knowledge = Rational Governance
Civilization requires both the enforcement of rules (W) and the wisdom to design them well (U).
Themes: Law, bureaucracy, order, jurisdiction, institution
Mechanics: Control, detain, flying, permission magic
Analogues: Constitutional democracies, international law

Dimir (U+B)

Ravnica: The Secret Guild — officially doesn't exist
Synthesis: Knowledge + Ambition = Strategic Manipulation
Information asymmetry is the ultimate power. Control what others believe.
Themes: Secrecy, espionage, mind control, information asymmetry
Mechanics: Mill, discard, surveil, cipher
Analogues: Intelligence agencies, corporate espionage

Rakdos (B+R)

Ravnica: The Cult — demonic circus; entertainment through mayhem
Synthesis: Ambition + Passion = Hedonistic Chaos
Pleasure with no moral constraints. Destruction as entertainment.
Themes: Chaos, hedonism, spectacle, violence as art
Mechanics: Direct damage, sacrifice, haste, chaos
Analogues: Extreme hedonism, nihilism, transgressive art

Gruul (R+G)

Ravnica: The Clans — exiled warriors in Ravnica's ruins
Synthesis: Passion + Nature = Primal Rage
Civilization is the disease; wildness is the cure. Ecology expressed through strength.
Themes: Wildness, rage, primitivism, tribal loyalty
Mechanics: Large creatures, trample, haste, bloodrush
Analogues: Anarcho-primitivism, ecological militancy

Selesnya (G+W)

Ravnica: The Conclave — nature-based spiritual community
Synthesis: Nature + Community = Communal Harmony
The greatest expression of nature is collective living rooted in shared purpose.
Themes: Community, nature-worship, unity, stewardship
Mechanics: Token generation, convoke, life gain, populate
Analogues: Eco-villages, religious communities, cooperatives

Enemy Guilds (Diagonal Pairs)

Colors two steps apart on the pentagon. Their philosophical tension generates the richest identities.

Orzhov (W+B)

Ravnica: The Syndicate — ghost-run banking and religion
Tension: Collectivism (W) vs. Individualism (B)
Resolution: Institutional Power — law as instrument of personal enrichment
Themes: Wealth, organized religion, debt, class hierarchy
Mechanics: Life drain, spirit tokens, extort, haunt

Izzet (U+R)

Ravnica: The League — eccentric mages led by dragon Niv-Mizzet
Tension: Calculation (U) vs. Impulse (R)
Resolution: Mad Science — each fuels the other; explosive brilliance
Themes: Innovation, experiments, creative chaos, overengineering
Mechanics: Instants/sorceries, overload, jump-start, storm

Golgari (B+G)

Ravnica: The Swarm — undead farmers, underground agriculture
Tension: Ambition (B) vs. Acceptance (G)
Resolution: Life-Death Cycle — decay IS the engine of renewal
Themes: Decay, scavenging, necromancy, abundance from rot
Mechanics: Graveyard recursion, scavenge, dredge, undergrowth

Boros (R+W)

Ravnica: The Legion — angelic military, zealous law enforcement
Tension: Freedom (R) vs. Order (W)
Resolution: Righteous Force — order enforced with burning conviction
Themes: Military, justice, angels, crusade, zeal
Mechanics: Aggressive creatures, battalion, mentor, haste

Simic (U+G)

Ravnica: The Combine — biomancers redesigning life
Tension: Progress (U) vs. Tradition (G)
Resolution: Guided Evolution — nature's potential refined by design
Themes: Bio-engineering, hybridization, guided evolution
Mechanics: +1/+1 counters, evolve, adapt, hybrid creatures

Artifact (Pseudo-Color)

Artifact — Colorless

Symbol: {C} · Geometry: Dodecahedron (12 pentagonal faces)

Artifacts are philosophically agnostic — pure technology divorced from any value position. A sword is neither White nor Black; only its use reveals a color identity. Any color can create or use artifacts, making them the universal language of the Color Pie.

ColorRelationship with Artifact
WhiteInstitutional tools — weapons of order
BlueStrongest affinity — artifact as intellect extension
BlackPower tools — weapons, necromantic machinery
RedAmbivalent — creates chaotically, destroys gleefully
GreenMost hostile — unnatural imposition on organic order

TSCG note: Artifact models a zero-philosophy substrate — pure Structure (S) with minimal Attractor (A). The material layer beneath the information layer of the Color Pie.
