■ S — Structure (block coupling)
■ F — Flow (value & cost)
■ It — Information (CS signals)
■ D — Dynamics (lifecycle)

Case Selector

Choose a real-world business model from the dropdown. Each case loads specific flux intensities and lifecycle parameters reflecting its actual configuration.

Phase Timeline

Click phase buttons or drag the progress bar to navigate the business model lifecycle. The morphism animates flux transitions between phases over ~2 seconds.

Cursors

VP Pull: Attractor strength — particle density on VP→CH→CS paths.
Conversion: CS→RS conversion rate — RS flow intensity.
CrossSubsidy: Subsidy flow between segments (platforms only).
Network FX: Multiplier on CS coupling (multi-sided platforms).
Cost Burden: KA+KR→C$ flow intensity vs RS.

Block Hover

Hover over any block to see its ASFID primary dimension, active M2 concepts, and current flux intensity.

Play / Pause

The player auto-advances through phases at the selected speed. During morphism, all flux paths interpolate simultaneously.
