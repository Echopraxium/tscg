System

Plate Tectonics — Earth's lithosphere mosaic of rigid plates floating on the viscous asthenosphere. The mantle is the engine.

Mantle Grid

The 3D grid in the asthenosphere (orange cells) is the interactive heart of the simulation. Mouse-over a cell → orange highlight Click a cell → gold + white wireframe + XYZ gizmo XYZ sliders → deform cell along each axis Apply → deformation propagates, stress increases Stress ≥ 95% → automatic earthquake

Deformation physics

Y+ uplift → upwelling, ridge push, plates diverge Y- subside → slab pull, plates converge X+ expand → lateral extension, divergence X- compress → lateral compression, convergence Z± → oblique/transform motion

3×3 Surface Plates

9 continental + 9 oceanic plate segments visible. Boundaries are fault zones accumulating stress.

Controls

Convection → heat flux → plate drift speed
Slab density → subduction angle
Asymmetry → Euler rotation of left plates
