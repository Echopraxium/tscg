⚠ Naming Inversion

BJT “Saturation” (switch fully ON) ≠ MOSFET “Saturation” (amplifier region). Both amplifier modes share blue to encode functional equivalence visually.

Processor Architecture

Transformation: I_B (μA) → I_C (mA)
Gain: β = I_C / I_B ≈ 100 (typical)
Power gain: A_p ≈ 10,000 (40 dB)

Key Facts (2N3904)

  • Current gain β: 100–300
  • I_C max: 200 mA  ·  V_CE max: 40 V
  • Switching speed: ~200 MHz (f_T)
  • V_BE threshold: ≈ 0.6–0.7 V

Historical Significance

Invented 1947 (Bardeen, Brattain, Shockley — Bell Labs). Nobel Prize 1956. >10²¹ transistors manufactured — most produced device in history.
