## System Description

### Core Principle
Yggdrasil maintains cosmic equilibrium through **perpetual circulation of energies** (waters from Hvergelmir, Ratatosk the messenger squirrel, wind) among seven interdependent poles. No pole is superior; each is essential to systemic cohesion.

### The Seven Poles

1. **Niflheim/Helheim** - Origins & Memory  
   - Function: Conservation, primordial memory, dissolution, recycling  
   - Energy: Static cold, latent memory, potentiality

2. **Svartalfheim/Nidavellir** - Formation & Modeling  
   - Function: Structuration, algorithmic processing, hidden architecture  
   - Energy: Compression, patient craftsmanship, pattern forging

3. **Muspellheim** - Impulse & Conflict  
   - Function: Primary energy, expansion, catalysis, creative destruction  
   - Energy: Fire, radiation, irresistible impulse

4. **Midgard/Ásgard/Vanaheim** - Manifestation & Order  
   - Function: Expression, law, exchange, organized consciousness  
   - Energy: Dynamic equilibrium, vertical sovereignty, organic cycles

5. **Jötunheim** - Wisdom & Resonance  
   - Function: Wild knowledge, chaotic-yet-fertile forces, ancient memory  
   - Energy: Telluric intelligence, cunning, cosmic scale

6. **Álfheim** - Inspiration & Fluidity  
   - Function: Creativity, lightness, intuition, ethereal connection  
   - Energy: Soft light, creative impulse, grace

7. **Tree Crown/Urd's Well** - Synthesis & Destiny  
   - Function: Global vision, fate, judgment, integration  
   - Energy: Impartiality, ultimate perspective, acceptance

### Dynamic Links

- **Ratatosk (Squirrel)**: Information flux between lowest (Nídhögg at roots) and highest (Eagle at crown) poles
- **Three Roots**: Functional conduits drawing from memory, conflict, and destiny sources
- **Perpetual Tension**: Tree constantly gnawed (Nídhögg) and restored (Nornes), embodying **Cyclic Tension** principle
